The Horse Show Services Company

The Horse Show Services Company is a Montreal-based provider in specialized workforce for national and international horseshows. Established in 2012, we were created to answer the urgent need of horse show organizers for competent ring crew and have since evolved to become a reference, providing men that perform a wide array of services all around Canada and now in the United States

Our teams

The Original Division, it was founded by CEO Gabriel Bélanger who saw the need to update the quality of the technicians in Canada.

The Quebec Team

The workforce, composed entirely of independent contractors, was hard to find and the turnover rate was enormous.

Born at the end of 2012, this team was nomad until it settled to the Caledon Equestrian Park, working from April until October.

The Ontario Team

The Ontario team is currently servicing major shows only and is expanding its activities into the Silver Circuit.

The Spruce Meadows division of HSS.Co has been in action since 2015 and has continued to evolve throughout the years.

The Alberta Team
